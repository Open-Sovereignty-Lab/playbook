Start here. This section establishes the principles that govern every decision in the Playbook, then walks through the three phases of putting them into practice.

## What This Section Covers

**[Module 0: Sovereignty in the Digital Age](/playbook/docs/introduction/module-0/)** is the foundation for everything that follows. Before we talk about AI tools, cloud platforms, procurement, or technical architecture, Module 0 establishes the principle that governs all of those decisions: tribal sovereignty is inherent, it is not granted, and it extends into every domain where tribal nations exercise self-governance, including the digital.

The work itself is organized into three phases:

- **[Phase 1: Pre-Work](/playbook/docs/introduction/phase-1/)** — preparing the tribe, its employees, and its citizens to use these tools responsibly.
- **[Phase 2: Execution](/playbook/docs/introduction/phase-2/)** — selecting, contracting for, and deploying AI and cloud technology.
- **[Phase 3: Monitoring and Evaluation](/playbook/docs/introduction/phase-3/)** — keeping deployed systems accountable to the tribe over time.

Once you are grounded in this section, the [Guides](/playbook/docs/guides/), [Templates](/playbook/docs/templates/), [Tools](/playbook/docs/tools/), and [Case Studies](/playbook/docs/case-studies/) provide the practical, reusable resources that support each phase.
